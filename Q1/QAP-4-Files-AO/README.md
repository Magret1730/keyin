## Insurance(Python) and Marina Projects(Javascript)

## Python Project - One Stop Insurance Company

This program calculates insurance premiums based on customer details,
cars insured, optional coverages, payment method, and past claims.

### Features:
- Collects customer information and coverage options.
- Calculates premiums with discounts, extra charges, HST, and monthly payments.
- Displays past claims.

### Constants:
- **Basic premium**: $869
- **Discount**: 25% for additional cars
- **Extra charges**: Liability, glass, loaner car
- **HST**: 15%
- **Processing fee**: $39.99
